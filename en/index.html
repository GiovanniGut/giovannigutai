The Process

From the written word
to sound

I was looking for a tool. I found a voice.

01

The Word

It begins with an idea, a verse, an image. The raw material is always human: words shaped by experience, silence, and intention.

02

The Dialogue

I bring the words, the AI translates them into new forms — sounds, melodies, textures. With every exchange, the words dig deeper. In this dialogue, the verses discover themselves: they change sound, and they change meaning.

03

The Depth

What emerges is neither the original poetry nor a machine-made production. It is something that has survived the encounter — words and sounds with a depth they didn't possess before. What you hear is the point where they met.

"I can generate formal beauty, but it is up to you to verify if it is authentic beauty."
— The AI, during the creation of Indigo Nostalgia
About

I am a poet. When I entrusted my verses to an artificial intelligence, something unexpected happened: to explain my words to it, I had to understand them better myself. From that dialogue, the music was born — not generated, but co-created.
